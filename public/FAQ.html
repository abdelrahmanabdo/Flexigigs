Employer FAQs:

How does Flexigigs work?

How does Flexigigs work?

Basically, Flexigigs is a platform designed for employers and freelance, part-time and intern service providers to meet. Employers register in order to be able to search FlexiGigs’ extensive database for the perfect candidate to perform a job for them.

How do I hire on flexigigs?

How do I hire on flexigigs?

It’s pretty simple. You, as a HeadHunter, can hire a service provider, a GigHunter, through one of the following methods:

  1. Direct order: search the database and lock an order with your chosen service provider.

  2. Post a Gig: publish an advertisement for service providers to apply for. You can then shortlist the applications and confirm the order with your chosen service provider.

How do I pay my service provider and what does it cost?

How do I pay my service provider and what does it cost?

This is the best part. Paying a service provider is processed through FlexiGigs. Once an order has been placed with a service provider, will make the payment in advance and in full. Money is then held in FlexiGigs’ super secure “cash vault” until the job is successfully done.

FlexiGigs offers you different payment methods for more convenience. You can either pay online using your credit cards, or through any “Fawry” outlet. No trips to the bank or awkward envelope slipping necessary. You will just be charged a premium of 10% over and above service cost in handling fees.

To avoid any scams or fraud, you have to notify FlexiGigs immediately if your service provider solicits payment from you outside the platform.

Is my personal information safe?

Is my personal information safe?

Relax, Flexigigs guarantees that any personal information provided will be handled securely.

Please refer to our “Privacy Policy” for more details.

What is FlexiGigs' "Cash Vault"?

What is FlexiGigs' "Cash Vault"?

The Cash Vault feature enables you to transfer certain payment responsibilities to FlexiGigs. This is where the money you’ve paid in advance is held- nice and cozy- to be released when your service provider has completed the service successfully.

What if I have a disagreement with my service provider?

What if I have a disagreement with my service provider?

Sad but true; in certain cases a dispute may occur between you and your chosen service provider. Flexigigs can assume the role of impartial mediator in order to resolve the issue for a reasonable fee.

Please refer to our “Refund Policy” for more details.

Why shouldn’t I pay my freelancer directly/offline?

Why shouldn’t I pay my freelancer directly/offline?

Well, to protect your rights of course! FlexiGigs is designed to uphold and reserve the rights of both employers and service providers. To avoid scams or any type of fraud, Flexigigs will keep the money- safe and sound- on behalf of both parties, until the job is successfully performed.

How does Flexigigs protect my rights as an employer?

How does Flexigigs protect my rights as an employer?

To ensure that you get exactly what you pay for, FlexiGigs agrees not to pay service providers for services until after you have accepted the completed service to your satisfaction.

How does Flexigigs calculate its fees?

How does Flexigigs calculate its fees?

Great news! Registration and posting jobs are completely free. The only fee you are responsible for is a 10% handling fee when you pay an invoice.

In case of a “Dispute Case”, Dispute Resolution Service Fee is equal to EGP 25 or 5% (whichever is greater) of the total amount you’ve paid to FlexiGigs for the project in question.

How do I get hired by clients?

How do I get hired by clients?

It’s simple. After uploading your portfolio, you, as a GigHunter, can get hired by an employer, a HeadHunter, through one of the following methods:

  1. Direct order: when an employer directly locks an order with you as a preferred service provider.

  2. Search gigs: when you search the database of published advertisements and apply for the one which fits your profile best. Your application will be sent to the employer, after which he will shortlist the applications and confirm the order with the chosen service provider (hopefully you!).

How does Flexigigs calculate its fees?

Great news! Registration and applying for jobs are completely free. You are only charged 10% out of the total project fee as a transaction fee. This amount will be deducted from your earnings. If you choose to be paid through bank transfer, then an additional 2% will be deducted as a bank transfer fee.

In case of a “Dispute Case”, Dispute Resolution Service Fee is equal to EGP 25 or 5% (whichever is greater) of the total amount the employer has paid to FlexiGigs for the project in question.

How does Flexigigs protect my rights as a freelancer?

FlexiGigs will accordingly assume responsibility for the payment, directly releasing it to you as soon as the employer verifies that the service has been successfully completed. No more late payments and lost invoices!

For more details, please refer to clause II “Safe Deposit Service” under section 6 “FlexiGigs BILLING AND PAYMENT SERVICES” of Terms and Conditions.
